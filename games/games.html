It's a simple casual "find a pair" game done in the style of tarot cards.

The game has three modes, differing in the number of rounds and the number of cards on the board. There is also a "Light Mode", which makes the game even easier: in this mode, the found cards are not turned over if you make a mistake.

Test your memory and attention by completing all rounds in one of the modes!

* Mobile support

This is a simple math merge game where you have to deactivate a bomb by hacking it.

To hack the bomb, you need to move the green area on the sum bar to the yellow area. The position of the area depends on the sum of all numbers on the playing field. Therefore, to move the green area in the right direction, it is necessary to decrease or increase the sum.

The game is quite complicated and confusing, but that is its peculiarity. Will you be able to successfully disarm the bomb and prevent it from exploding? Let's check it out!
